EXPLORE VR & AR

Virtual Reality

It is a fully immersive, computer-generated environment that completely replaces the user's real-world view typically requiring a bulky headset for total sensory isolation.

Augmented Reality

It is a conversely, superimposes digital content and information onto the user's existing physical world, allowing them to remain fully present in their surroundings.
